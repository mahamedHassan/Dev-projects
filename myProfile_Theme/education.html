My Education 🔇

Msc. - Computer Science

The programme focuses on the realisation of innovative IT services and systems. You will learn about the technologies and concepts essential for the development of modern information systems, which are typically complex and include cooperative components. Such technologies and concepts include software agents, service-oriented computing, middleware and ontologies. You will also learn how these technologies can be applied to business systems and other support activities that are critical to society.

Bsc.Web - Development

The Top-up Bachelor's degree in Web Development is a 1½-year study program offered to graduates from the AP degree in Computer Science or the AP degree in Multimedia Design and Communication. The program includes an internship in a company abroad or in Denmark. The internship will give you the opportunity to use the competences that you have gained in class and put them into practice.

Assoc. Degree - Computer Programming Language

The AP degree programme in computer science is a higher education degree that takes 2 years to complete as a full-time course of study.
